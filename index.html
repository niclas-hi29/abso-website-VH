07 The formulator
Why a 2 mg melatonin dose in Sleep?

Melatonin cues your circadian phase — the "it's night now" signal. The common 5–10 mg gummy doses are far above what the body uses and tend to leave people groggy. 2 mg is enough to cue the shift, low enough to wake clear-headed, paired with valerian, L-theanine, lavender, passion flower and GABA.

Source · Cell Reports Medicine, 2023 · NIH PMC
Is sublingual actually better?

For the molecules we use, meaningfully. Absorbing under the tongue gives roughly 70–90% bioavailability versus ~30–50% swallowed, and it bypasses first-pass liver metabolism — the same physiology behind clinical sublingual films.

Source · Br. J. Clinical Pharmacology, 2018
Why hold yourselves to five minutes?

If you can't feel a strip within five minutes, it isn't working — there's nowhere to hide a sub-clinical dose. Most supplements are sold on hope. We'd rather be judged on the next 300 seconds.
